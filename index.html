Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals to the right. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your final score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, and you lose the game by going over it.


The value of each crystal is hidden, but can be determined through simple arithmetic.


Each time the game starts, a new random number is chosen.
